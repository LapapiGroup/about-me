How i started my journey

I am an aspiring software developer passionate about writing clean, efficient code and solving real-world problems through technology. Currently pursuing studies in software development, I am developing strong foundations in programming, software design, and system thinking.

My journey into software development was not something I planned from the beginning. Like many students, I went through a period of uncertainty about what career path to choose. I explored different interests and faced moments of confusion about my future. During that time, I realized that I was always curious about how technology works. I was fascinated by mobile apps, websites, and computer systems. I started asking myself: *How are these things built? Who creates them?* That curiosity led me to start learning about programming. At first, it was challenging. I struggled to understand concepts like variables, loops, and object-oriented programming. There were moments when I felt frustrated and doubted myself. However, each small success fixing a bug, completing a small project, understanding a difficult concept gave me motivation to continue. Over time, I discovered that software development is not just about writing code. It is about solving problems, thinking logically, and building solutions that can help people. I realized I enjoy the process of learning, experimenting, and improving step by step.
